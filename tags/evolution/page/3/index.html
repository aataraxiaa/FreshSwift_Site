Date.now() and other calendar thoughts

Dave Delong started this interesting discussion on the Swift Forums. In Dave’s opinion, the current date/time APIs of Foundation are lacking, and adding a suggested Date.now() function will only make things worse. Dave then proposes a related addition to Foundation, Clock, which would tell us the date and time of day.

Introducing Related Projects to Swift Forums

As always, it’s great to see the core Swift team recognize the Swift community and it’s efforts, and in this official Swift blog post Ted kremenek announces an expansion to the Swift Forums. A new top-level category, Related Projects, is being added to the Swift Forum, creating a space for discussions relating to Swift projects developed by the community. Great stuff!
